-> Dart language is focused on frontend (mobile, web apps) user interface (UI) development.

-> Dart was developed by Google in 2011. It is an object oriented and strongly typed language. 

-> It is a mixture of JavaScript, Java and C#.

--- BENEFITS OF DART ---
- Use of async and await for asynchronous programming.
- Dart is one of very few languages that is well suited to being compiled both AOT(Ahead-of-time) and JIT(Just-in-time).
